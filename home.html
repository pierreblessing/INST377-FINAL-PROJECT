FaQs

How do I use the weather website?

To use our website, locate the weather page. In this page, you will be able to enter a zipcode, location, or city.

What information does the weather page give us?

The weather page gives you information such as; the weather condition, wind speed, humidity, and the high and low of the day

I am getting an error when trying to load the weather for a location. what should i do?

If you are experiencing trouble, either check to make sure you entered a proper parameter for your search or reload the webpage.
